MIRING Validator for HML

Paste some XML, compliant with HML 1.0.1 into the HML field and hit "VALIDATE".

MIRING results will appear below.

Or, use the "Choose File" button to browse for an HML document on your local computer.

The MIRING HML Validator is a RESTful service, available to the public, hosted at miring.b12x.org/validator/ValidateMiring/

You can also validate using a CURL command to issue a POST request, like this:

$ curl -X POST --data-urlencode 'xml=<hml>...</hml>' http://miring.b12x.org/validator/ValidateMiring/
